(Don't worry — this was a training exercise.)

The page you just signed into was a fake. If this had been a real attack, your email and password would now be in the hands of a criminal.

What gave it away (or should have):

  • The URL in the address bar — was it really your company's domain?
  • The email that brought you here — did you check the sender?
  • Urgency cues like "mailbox almost full" pressuring you to act fast
  • No multi-factor authentication step

What to do next time:

  • Hover over links before clicking
  • Type the URL yourself rather than clicking email links
  • Turn on MFA everywhere
  • Report suspicious emails to IT
